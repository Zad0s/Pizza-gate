Intro

On May 22, 2010, a seemingly ordinary event occurred: someone exchanged Bitcoin for two pizzas. At the time, those pizzas were nothing special, but today they have become culinary icons of the crypto world. Those two pizzas should have been framed instead of devoured because they are now worth a fortune. Imagine taking a bite of pizza and realizing it's like biting into molten gold... or at least into a ridiculously valuable amount of Bitcoin. The lessons to be learned? Never underestimate the power of pizza, and don't eat your investments!

How to Buy

Create a Wallet

Download MetaMask or another wallet of your choice from the app store or Google Play Store for free. For desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension MetaMask.

Get Some BNB

Have BNB in your wallet to switch to $PIZZA. If you don't have any BNB, you can buy it on an exchange and transfer it to your wallet.

Go to PancakeSwap

Go to www.pancakeswap.finance/swap in Google Chrome or on the browser inside your wallet. Connect your wallet. Paste the $PIZZA token address into PancakeSwap, select CatDog, and confirm. When MetaMask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Switch BNB for $PIZZA

We have 5% taxes, so you need to set slippage. Although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
